Facts about VIT

  • Over 1,93,000 appeared for the VIT Engineering Entrance Examination (VITEEE) in 2014
  • The institution offers 19 Undergraduate, 34 Post graduate, 2 Integrated and 4 Research Programmes.
  • NAAC has re-accredited VIT University with an 'A' grade.
  • A 360-acre eco-friendly campus with over 50.83 lakh sq.ft. built-up space at Vellore.
  • A 170 acre eco-friendly second campus at Chennai.
  • Over 24,000 students, with nearly 1/3rd of them women
  • Students from 50 countries as well as from every state in India
  • Over 1400 faculty and 1413 staff.
  • VIT has been accredited by TCS, Wipro and Cognizant for academic collaboration and placements.
  • Entered the Limca Book of Records for Campus Recruitment.
